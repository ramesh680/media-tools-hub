Artist 100 - New Entries (LW = "-")

Scrapes the Billboard Artist 100 chart and gives you an Excel file of just the artists whose Last Week (LW) value is a dash "-" - i.e. acts that are new to the chart or re-entering with no prior-week position.

How it works

  • Leave the date blank for the latest chart.
  • Or pick any date - Billboard serves that week's chart.
  • You get an .xlsx: Rank, Artist, LW, Peak, Weeks, Chart Date, IMDb nmcode + URL, Wikipedia URL, Billboard URL.
  • IMDb & Wikipedia are looked up live via Wikidata; artists without a match are left blank.
