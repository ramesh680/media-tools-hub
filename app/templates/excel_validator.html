Active validation rules

Required Columns

title, title_category, title_sub_category, genre, primary_genre, companies, brand_set, facebook_page

DAR / Competitive

DAR titles require Pristine company values and Pristine DAR Brands. Non-DAR titles require Competitive View.

Movie / TV Checks

IMDb ttcode and Metacritic URL suggestions are fetched from the row title. IMDb title/name datasets and IMDb title pages drive genre, release date, ttcode, and network/distributor checks.

YouTube

YouTube channel company values are checked with the configured API key when available.

Wikipedia

wikipedia_url must use en.wikipedia.org/wiki and the article name must match the row title.

URL Managers

url_managers must include row companies and social accounts; Unknown and Pristine Brand are ignored from companies for this check.
